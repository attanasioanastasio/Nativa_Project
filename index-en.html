A Journey Begins

Nativa is not just a project but a true spiritual search. A path starting in 2025, born from three friends’ desire to rekindle the deep, ancestral bond between humans and the land.

Our mission is to explore the most ethereal and ancient part of distillation — the alchemical principle hidden within plants. We believe alcohol is a matrix, a beating heart that links the material to the immaterial, turning every distillation into a ritual that captures the pure essence of nature.

Our approach pays homage to artisanal tradition, combined with constant research. Every plant is a story, every distillation a chapter, and the whole process is driven by a profound passion for the vegetal world.

The First Chapter: Magnolia Gin

The eternal flower of the soul, turned into a unique spirit.

Magnolia Gin

Magnolia is our first tribute to this philosophy. A London Dry Gin born from the desire to bottle nature’s elegance and strength. Its recipe is a harmonious composition that evokes a Mediterranean, floral sensory landscape — a liquid expression of a secret garden.

Crafted using the traditional London Dry method, every distillation is a ritual in copper stills. This is where true alchemy happens, transforming selected botanicals into a liquid that holds their purest essence.
