Magnolia Gin

The eternal flower of the soul, turned into a unique spirit.

Magnolia is our first tribute to this philosophy. A London Dry Gin born from the desire to bottle elegance and strength of nature, a liquid expression of a secret garden.

London Dry Gin Method
43.2% vol - 70cl
100% Sicilian botanicals
Limited edition

The Ritual of Distillation

Produced using the traditional London Dry method, each distillation is a ritual in copper stills. Botanicals are infused in a high-quality alcohol base and then distilled to extract and capture their purest essence.

The meticulous separation of “heads” and “tails” ensures that only the most noble and aromatic part remains in the heart of the distillate. This is the moment of true alchemy, where plants are transformed into a liquid embodying their essence.

Our Botanicals

A balance of flavors and aromas: the heart of our recipe.

Magnolia Flower

The distinctive element giving delicate yet persistent floral notes. Distillation is carefully managed to extract a balanced aroma.

Sicilian Juniper

The indispensable base of every London Dry Gin. We select premium juniper berries to ensure the classic balsamic and resinous notes that give structure.

Coriander

Seeds provide a spicy, citrus component, fundamental for balancing freshness and adding complexity to the bouquet.

Thyme

Thyme adds an herbal and balsamic note, a bridge between floral aromas and earthy, resinous juniper notes.

Iris

A precious root that adds an earthy, slightly floral note while acting as a natural “fixative”, ensuring persistence of aromas over time.

Cucumber

Its distillation captures a delicate vegetal freshness, lightening the palate and making the gin incredibly refreshing.

Bergamot

The zest adds vibrant acidity and a unique citrus-floral scent, essential for brightness.

Prickly Pear

An original addition that releases delicate sweetness and Mediterranean warmth.

The Mixing Experience

Magnolia is not just a gin, but an experience. With its 43.2% ABV, it offers a firm, chewy body that wraps the palate with persistent warmth, making it extremely versatile.
